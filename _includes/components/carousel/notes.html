For videos you also need:
  • <script src="//web-toolkit.global.sky.com/static/lib/require-2.1.9.min.js"></script>
  • <link rel="stylesheet" href="//player.sky.com/css/styles.min.css" />
  • <script src="//player.sky.com/plugin/jquery.sky.htmlplayer.js"></script>

For tracking image clicks there are a possibility of two places you might need to place data-tracking attribute. Either on the shade div if you are using the bg-shading. or on the image tag if you have no shading.
